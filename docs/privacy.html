Privacy Policy

Splitwork is an offline-first app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device in the app's Application Support directory and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

We do not collect analytics, do not use tracking identifiers, and do not require an account. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit; we never see your payment details.

If you delete the app, your local data is deleted with it. There is no server-side copy.
